2.3 Adding and Styling the Heading
Continue recreating the header by adding a new row and cell to style the heading text.
	
	- Add a new tr with a td containing the text Championship Match!
	- Set the color of the text on the td to #3a383a.
	- Style the heading text using a font-family of Georgia, serif; and a font-size of 42px;.
	- Make the heading bold and transform the text to uppercase.
	- Apply spacing to the heading using a line-height of 44px and padding-top of 20px.
